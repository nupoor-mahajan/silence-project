About the Project

Why silence itself is a governance signal

Problem Statement

Most governance systems track complaints, but they often ignore places where complaints are missing. Low complaint volume does not always mean peace — it may reflect fear, lack of access, or suppression.

Objectives

  • Identify unusually silent districts using complaint density.
  • Highlight regions needing attention despite low reporting.
  • Generate analytics dashboards and ranked silent-zone lists.
  • Support ethical governance decision-making.
